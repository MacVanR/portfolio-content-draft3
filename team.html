Team Logistics

We traditionally practice every Sunday, Monday, and Wednesday. Weekend pracitce are 12am-2pm and weekdays 9pm-11pm. These times allow us to have our needed time together while not interfering with other academic and social commitments. Times are subject to change week to week based on team needs.



All players are expected to attend atleast two out of three practices. Carpools are availible for practices and games. Living on North Campus is not a problem! Have of our games are in our home gym, the Sports Colluseum and half are away. Drives to away games are never over 6 hours. Finally, every year the team spends a weekend in Chicago during our away game at Depaul University.
